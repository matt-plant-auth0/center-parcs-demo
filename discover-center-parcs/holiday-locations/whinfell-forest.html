        Discover picturesque landscapes at Whinfell Forest, a wildlife sanctuary nestled on the edge of the Lake District. Experience a range of activities, restaurants and traditional accommodation, all set around a beautiful lake.

        • Look out for endangered red squirrels and the uncommon orchid
        • Wander amongst tall pines and trickling streams
        • Try Go Karts and our Golf Driving Range, unique to Whinfell Forest
        • Explore our largest Subtropical Swimming Paradise
        • Enjoy a traditional family pub meal at Lakeside Inn overlooking the lake
        • Stay in traditional, Scandinavian-style lodges, tucked away amongst the trees

        All our villages are cashless and can no longer accept cash payments, including for the purchase of gift cards.

        What to expect when you arrive

        Find out what to do when you arrive for your break, from the check-in process and where to park to what you can do on your first day.

        Arriving for your break

        From 10am onwards, you can enter the village. Follow the signs to the Arrivals Lodge, where you’ll be given a village map and a wristband, which acts as a key for your accommodation and for lockers in the Subtropical Swimming Paradise. You can access your accommodation from 4pm (unless you’ve booked early arrival or have a Treehouse or Exclusive Lodge) – so in the meantime, why not go swimming, explore the village or enjoy a meal in one of our restaurants?

        Read what to do on arrival

        Parking

        Center Parcs is car-free, which helps us to maintain a safe and peaceful environment where guests, including little ones, can explore the forest on foot or by cycle. If you arrive by car you’ll need to leave it in the car park for the majority of your stay, with some exceptions:

        • From 4pm on your arrival day, you can drive to your lodge to unpack your belongings
        • Before 10am on your departure day, you can drive to your lodge to pack your belongings
        • Guests in adapted lodges, Treehouses and Exclusive lodges can leave cars outside the lodge
        • Center Parcs maintenance staff and restaurant delivery drivers do drive around the village

        If you have an electric vehicle, you may be able to pre-book an Electric Vehicle charging space (some villages don’t require you to pre-book). Check this when booking.

        Settling in 

        Get your break started in style with our top tips for settling in.

        • Head to the Cycle Centre and pick up pre-booked cycles for you and your family.
        • Pick up your essentials from ParcMarket. If you spend £40, we’ll deliver it to your lodge for you free of charge!
        • You can enter the village from 10am on your arrival day. Make the most of every moment of your break and take a dip in the Subtropical Swimming Paradise, enjoy an activity or simply explore the village before checking in to your lodge!

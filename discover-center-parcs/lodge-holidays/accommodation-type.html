        Do the lodges come with Wi-Fi?

        Yes, our complimentary Free Friendly Wi-Fi is available across the entire village throughout your stay.

        Do the lodges have shaver plugs in the bathroom?

        Yes, all lodges are equipped with shaver plugs.

        Do I get a daily housekeeping service?

        We do not offer a daily housekeeping service.Â 

        Do we get a change of towels?

        Unfortunately, we don't change towels during your short break. You will find extra towels in the lodge.

        Can I park outside my lodge?

        While you can park outside your lodge to unload your car, it must be returned to the main car park by 10pm on your arrival day.

        What time do I have to check out of my lodge?

        You must leave your lodge by 10am, but you are welcome to park your car back in the car park and stay on the village until midnight.

        Can I bring my pets?

        Unfortunately not, unless you've booked a dog friendly lodge and prebooked your four-legged friend in. No other pets are allowed.

        Are the lodges baby friendly?

        Yes. Our lodges are all baby-friendly, specially designed to minimise stress and ensure you have everything you need. Each lodge includes a cot (linen not included) and a highchair.

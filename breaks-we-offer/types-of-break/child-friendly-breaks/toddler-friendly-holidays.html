        Toddler friendly holidays and breaks in the UK

        Center Parcs is the perfect place for family holidays with toddlers in the UK. Discover acres of woodland for little feet to explore, hundreds of new and exciting indoor and outdoor activities for toddlers, and a wide variety of child-friendly restaurants and menus. Capture every moment with special photo opportunities found around the village and make Center Parcs your new family tradition. 

        Find your home-away-from-home in one of our specially designed open-plan family lodges and make memories that will last a lifetime.

        Making the most of your break with your toddler

        Here are our top tips on making the most of your break with your toddler at Center Parcs.

        • Book early arrival to beat the queues and get into your lodge quicker
        • Hire a pushchair from the Cycle Centre to save room in your car
        • Hire a trailer from the Cycle Centre for fun rides around the village
        • Select a lodge in a central area to be closer to the amenities
        • Pre-order groceries before you arrive and we will deliver them to your lodge ready for you to start your break
        • Our lodges have highchairs and cots, but remember to bring your own bed linen
        • Check your village map to see the locations of the indoor and outdoor play areas
        • Don’t forget to bring wellies and waterproofs for wet weather
        • If you forget anything, ParcMarket stocks plenty of essentials including swim nappies
        • Let little ones run wild in our Adventure Playgrounds with something exciting for all ages to explore

        Read top tips from our guests

        Toddler friendly lodges

        Our lodges are designed with families in mind and include everything you need for a relaxing break with a toddler. Whether you’re staying in a Woodland Lodge, a Treehouse or anything in between, you’ll find:

        • A cot (you’ll need to bring your own linen)
        • A highchair
        • A super-sized storage cupboard for your pushchair
        • A spacious layout, perfect for little explorers
        • Stair-gates (in two-storey lodges)
        • Forest views so little ones can spot wildlife

        Please be aware that some lodges have steps to the entrance, so check this when booking.

        Eating out on holiday with a toddler

        All of our restaurants are child-friendly. You’ll find plenty of space for multiple highchairs and pushchairs, children’s menus offering healthy and tasty dishes, and activity packs to keep little ones entertained. We also offer free Ella’s Kitchen baby food (subject to availability) as well as indoor or outdoor play areas at some of our restaurants. Be sure to pre-book your table as early evening times are popular.

         If you’d prefer a relaxing evening in, order restaurant food to be delivered to your lodge.

        Discover restaurants

        Take your toddler for a swim

        Splash into the Subtropical Swimming Paradise on a toddler-friendly break, heated to 29.5c all year round and home to children’s water play areas, splash pools and mini slides. Why not sing and splash along in our free Baby Dolphins play session? You’ll also find family changing rooms, large lockers, a buggy park and the Canopy Café and Bar serving poolside food and drink.

        Top tip: hire a Family Cabana for a cosy, peaceful retreat – perfect for naptime!

        Discover Subtropical Swimming Paradise

        Explore the forest

        The forest is a gigantic adventure playground and the ideal place to explore on a short break with toddlers. Splash through puddles, spot ducklings by the lake, look for creepy-crawlies under logs and enjoy the sights, sounds and smells of the forest around you. You can hire buggy trailers from the Cycle Centre – be sure to check your village map for the cycle routes. 

        Please be aware that due to the nature of the forest, not all woodland paths are suitable for pushchairs.

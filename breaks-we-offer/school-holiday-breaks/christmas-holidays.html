        Family Christmas activities

        Whatever your age, there’s sure to be something to get you into the festive spirit - from designing your very own Christmas stocking to discovering the magical village on our Festive Treasure Trail. Remember to pre-book your activities to avoid disappointment. Please note that activities are an additional cost. Some festive activities are available only up until Christmas Eve.

        Santa's Woodland Village

        At the heart of our Christmas breaks is Santa’s Woodland Village, covered with a sprinkling of snow. It’s free to enter and you can visit as often as you like. Inside, you’ll find:

        • Our new Toadstool Cabins, featuring elves in Christmas scenes
        • Our famous singing reindeer singing your favourite festive tunes
        • Seasonal sculptures and photo opportunities to capture that perfect family picture
        • The Forest Festive Fayre, where you can warm up with delicious festive drinks and treats (UK only)
        • Santa’s Woodland Workshop, with pre-bookable slots to see Santa up until Christmas Eve
        • Playful elves wandering round the snowy paths, showing off dance moves and magic tricks

        The Enchanted Light Garden

        This festive season we’re lighting up the forest with a brand-new free Enchanted Light Garden. Watch the little ones look up in awe as you walk through thousands of twinkling lights nestled between the trees and the forest floor. Lose yourself in a magical winter wonderland, surrounded by captivating festive tunes that will have you jingling and dancing along. You can visit every evening of your break. 

        Christmas Lunch

        Hang up your cooking apron and head to one of our restaurants for a traditional Christmas Lunch with all the trimmings. With special children’s menus, little ones can enjoy festive food on the big day too.
